Healthcare Training for Cattle Management

Welcome to the Healthcare Training for Cattle Management! Cattle healthcare is a critical component of successful and sustainable cattle management systems. Ensuring the health and well-being of your cattle not only promotes optimal productivity but also contributes to the overall success of your operation. This training program is designed to equip individuals involved in cattle management with the essential knowledge and skills needed to maintain a healthy and thriving herd.

Training Objectives:

  • Understanding Cattle Health
  • Preventive Healthcare Measures
  • Reproductive Health
  • Disease Identification and Treatment
  • Record Keeping and Health Monitoring
